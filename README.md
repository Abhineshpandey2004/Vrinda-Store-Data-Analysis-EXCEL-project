# Vrinda-Store-Data-Analysis-EXCEL-project
Vrinda Store performed well in 2022 with strong sales in the early months and steady performance throughout the year. Telangana and Delhi emerged as key markets, while Amazon remained a highly reliable sales channel. The customer base is evenly split between men and women, showing a wide market reach.
Overall, 2022 was a stable year with good delivery performance and healthy customer engagement.
